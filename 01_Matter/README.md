# 01_Matter

The case file. Static facts about the matter live here.

## Files to create as the matter develops

- `case_file.md` parties, caption, court, cause number, key facts, claims pleaded, damages claimed
- `case_theory.md` Lead Counsel's one paragraph theory (owned by Seat 01)
- `timeline.md` chronology of events from accident through filing
- `parties.md` plaintiff identity, plaintiff's counsel, plaintiff witnesses, plaintiff insurers
- `key_facts.md` admitted facts, contested facts, defendant's strongest facts, defendant's worst facts
- `deadlines.md` master deadline calendar (owned by Seat 15 Watchdog)
- `plaintiff_playbook.md` red-team output from Seat 18, living document

## Convention

Top of every file: caption, last-updated date, owner seat number.

Mark "ATTORNEY WORK PRODUCT (SELF PREPARED) / NOT FOR DISCLOSURE" at the top of any internal-only document.
