# 03_Discovery

Discovery materials in and out.

## Subfolders to create as the matter develops

- `outgoing/` discovery the defendant serves on plaintiff
- `incoming/` discovery the plaintiff serves on defendant
- `responses/` defendant's responses
- `motions/` motions for leave (TRCP 500.9), motions to compel, motions to quash, protective orders

## Texas authority quick reference

- TRCP 500.9 (J.P. Court discovery on leave only)
- TRCP 194 (request for disclosure)
- TRCP 196 (RFPs)
- TRCP 197 (interrogatories)
- TRCP 198 (RFAs, deemed-admitted clock)
- TRCP 215 (sanctions)
- TRE 503 (attorney-client)
- TRCP 192.5 (work product)
