# 04_Evidence

Physical and digital evidence inventory and source files.

## Subfolders

- `photos/` scene photos, vehicle photos, damage photos
- `dashcam/` dashcam clips and metadata
- `repair_estimates/` plaintiff's and defendant's
- `police_report/` the CR-3 (and our motion in limine to exclude under Tex. Transp. Code § 550.065)
- `medical/` medical records and bills (if any)
- `social_media/` plaintiff's public posts (no fake friending; see Seat 11 + Seat 17)
- `vehicle_history/` CARFAX, AutoCheck, NMVTIS reports
- `witness_statements/` written or recorded
- `communications/` texts, voicemails, emails, demand letters

## Inventory file

`inventory.md` lists every exhibit with source, custodian, date acquired, and TRE 901 authentication path.

## Texas authority quick reference

- TRE 901 (authentication)
- TRE 902 (self-authentication; § 902(10) self-proving business records affidavit)
- TRE 803(6) (business records)
- TRE 1002 (best evidence)
- Tex. Transp. Code § 550.065 (police report bar)
