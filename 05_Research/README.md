# 05_Research

Paralegal-driven case law research, rule cards, and form bank.

## Subfolders

- `memos/` per legal question, with controlling Texas authority
- `cite_checks/` cite-check log per panel deliverable
- `rules_cards/` quick reference cards on TRCP 500 to 510, TRE workhorses, CPRC Ch. 16, 18, 33, Tex. Transp. Code Ch. 550
- `forms/` Texas-specific motion, answer, and discovery templates

## Owner

Seat 20 Senior Paralegal: Case Law Research & Precedents.

## Discipline

- Every cited case is KeyCited before being relied on
- Texas Bluebook citation form for any document filed with the court
- Reversed, distinguished, or limited cases are flagged
- The cite-check log lives in `cite_checks/` and is updated for every deliverable
