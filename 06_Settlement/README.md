# 06_Settlement

Negotiation and settlement workspace.

## Anticipated documents

- `valuation_model.md` E[exposure] math (owned by Seat 12 Settlement Counsel)
- `demand_log.md` every plaintiff demand with date, amount, terms
- `offers_log.md` every defendant offer with date, amount, terms
- `rule_11_drafts/` TRCP 11 agreement drafts (signed Rule 11 agreements must be filed or read into record to be enforceable)
- `release_drafts/` full release drafts with indemnity, confidentiality, non-disparagement, no admission
- `mediation_prep.md` if mediation is set under TRCP 500.7

## Owner

Seat 12 Settlement & Mediation Counsel; coordinates with Seat 13 Insurance & Subrogation Counsel on subrogation carve-out language.

## Texas authority quick reference

- TRCP 11 (written agreements)
- TRCP 500.7 (J.P. Court ADR)
- CPRC § 154.001 et seq. (ADR generally)
