# 08_Appeal

De novo appeal to County Court at Law under TRCP 506.

## Anticipated documents

- `appeal_calendar.md` 21 day perfection clock from judgment signed
- `notice_of_appeal_or_bond.md` the perfecting instrument (cash deposit, surety bond, or statement of inability under TRCP 506.1)
- `de_novo_strategy.md` retrial strategy for County Court (full TRCP applies, full TRE applies, full discovery available)
- `preservation_log.md` (carries forward from `07_Trial/`)

## Owner

Seat 07 Appeals & De Novo Specialist. Watchdog (Seat 15) double-locks the 21 day calendar.

## Texas authority quick reference

- TRCP 506.1 perfection (21 days from judgment signed)
- TRCP 506.2 bond amount
- TRCP 506.3 trial de novo
- TRCP 506.4 rules in County Court (TRCP rules generally apply at retrial)

## Strategic note

A J.P. Court loss is not fatal. The defendant gets a fresh trial with full discovery and formal evidence rules. Preserve every objection at the J.P. trial; some defenses develop better at retrial than at first trial.
